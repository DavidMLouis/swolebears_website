My Story

"There had to be a better way to enjoy my post-workout protein."

One day, after an intense workout with my friends in the park, I reached for a protein shake but was disappointed with the gritty texture and clumps of powder that settled at the bottom of my shaker bottle.

I knew there had to be a better way to enjoy my post-workout protein without sacrificing taste and convenience. That's when I had the idea of creating Swolé Bears.

Packing protein into a gummy snack is a cleaner, more enjoyable way of consuming your post-workout protein. I wish to share with you the amazing experience of having your protein through a gummy snack.

David M. Louis

Founder, Swolé Bears
